// Know Your Deadlines

Miss the window. Pay the penalty.

Form 720 is filed quarterly. Miss a deadline and you face a 5% per month late-filing penalty up to 25%, plus a ½% per month late-payment penalty up to 25% — plus interest. EasyFile720 tracks your quarter and alerts you well in advance.

⚠ Note for Form 7208: The stock repurchase excise tax is filed annually — attached to the Form 720 due in the first full quarter after your tax year closes. A December fiscal year-end means Form 7208 is due April 30.
💡 PCOR Fee Exception: The Patient-Centered Outcomes Research fee is filed once per year on the 2nd Quarter Form 720 only — due July 31. We handle this routing automatically.
